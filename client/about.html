About

Help Me Budget is a tool to help families budget their earnings and view a visual representation of what they are spending. The software also makes suggestions on how a user should spend their money based on historical data from various sources (see source).

Our mission is to help low income families or families without experience in finance manage their money by showing them what they are spending and giving them suggestions. How it works

Getting started with Help Me Budget is simple! To receive a personalized report, anyone can enter the following information:

  • How often you are paid
  • The amount you are paid
  • The number of people in your household
  • How much you spend in different categories

That's it! Our software will then generate a personalized report that you can use to analyze your savings and view our recommendations.
